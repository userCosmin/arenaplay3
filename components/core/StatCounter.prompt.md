Animated count-up stat for hero/proof strips — counts up once on scroll into view.

```jsx
<StatCounter value={16} label="Jucători simultan" tone="accent" />
<StatCounter value={50} suffix="+" label="Jocuri VR" />
<StatCounter value={3} suffix=" ore" label="Pachet petrecere" tone="secondary" />
```

Use real venue figures. `tone` colors the number; `prefix`/`suffix` for units.
