Pricing tile for walk-in sessions and party/event packages.

```jsx
<PriceCard title="VR Dinamic · XR" price={30} per="sesiune"
  period="20 min joc + 10 min igienizare" features={["Free-roam wireless","Multiplayer până la 16"]} />
<PriceCard title="Pachet petrecere" price={100} per="persoană" featured badge="Popular"
  features={["3 ore acces exclusiv","Pizza 1/2 copii","Invitație digitală"]} cta="Rezervă petrecere" />
```

`featured` adds the violet glow + ribbon. Use real prices (sessions 30 lei, party 100 lei/pers).
